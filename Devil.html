What is Health and hygiene ?

Hygiene can be outlined as the practice of a few habits in order to maintain good health, overall. Maintenance of hygiene can be at the community level (social hygiene) or personal level (personal hygiene).Health is a state of complete well being both physically and mentally. A healthy person is one whose mind and body are fit12. Hygiene is a set of habits or practices that ensure good health and a clean environment. Hygiene includes behaviors such as frequent handwashing,personal cleaning. A balanced and nutritious diet is a prerequisite for good health. Fruits and vegetables have vital minerals and vitamins which we need. We must wash fruits and cook vegetables before consumption. Hygiene refers to conditions and practises that help to maintain health and prevent the spread of diseases

Symptoms and indicators of disease emerge as a result of these changes. On the basis of the symptoms, doctors will check for signs of disease. Signs will provide a more definitive indication of the presence of a disease. Physicians also order laboratory testing in order to narrow down the disease's cause.

Common Cold:

It is caused by rhinovirus Affects the nose, bronchioles, and lungs. It usually lasts 4-5 days. Sore throat, nasal congestion, headache, and cough are all common symptoms. Spreads by contact with infected things and droplets from sneezing and coughing

Hepatitis (Jaundice) :

It is caused by virus HAV (Hepatitis A Virus) It affects the liver High fever, loss of appetite, headache, dark yellow urine, light coloured stool are common symptoms. It spreads by the way of contaminated water

Ringworm:

It is caused by fungi Microsporum and Trichophyton It affects the skin, fingernails and scalp Itching on skin and scalp, dry scaly lesions on skin, nails and scalp are common symptoms. It spreads by the way of direct contact with infectious objects

Malaria:

It is caused by many species of Plasmodium. It affects the liver and spleen. High fever accompanied with chills, enlargement of liver and spleen, loss of appetite, headache are common symptoms. It spreads by the way of the bite of female Anopheles mosquito that can transfer the sporozoites of Plasmodium from an infected person to a normal person.
